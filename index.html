Specializations
Clinical Services

Comprehensive urological and reproductive care using the latest evidence-based protocols.

🔬
HoLEP & Laser Prostate Surgery
Holmium laser enucleation for large-gland BPH. Minimally invasive with faster recovery and lower recurrence rates.
⚕️
Male Reproductive Surgery
Microsurgical varicocelectomy, vasectomy reversal, sperm retrieval (PESA/TESE), and male infertility workup.
🏥
Urological Oncology
Diagnosis and management of bladder, prostate, kidney, and testicular cancers.
🧪
Diagnostic Workup & STI
Comprehensive laboratory investigations across bacterial, viral, and parasitic urological pathogens.
💧
Neurogenic Bladder & Voiding
Evaluation and management of neurogenic bladder, overactive bladder, urinary retention, and incontinence.
🌐
International Consultations
Serving patients from Yemen, Gulf states, and across the Arab world. Remote consultation available.
🪨
Urinary Stones — Kidney, Ureter & Bladder
Comprehensive stone management including flexible ureteroscopy with laser lithotripsy (FURS), rigid endoscopic removal, laser fragmentation (Ho:YAG / Thulium), and classical endoscopic surgery for all stone locations.
